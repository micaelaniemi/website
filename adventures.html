Adventures

I love the outdoors!!! When I was a kid, I would read books about the outdoors, animals, fiction and non-fiction.

At a young age, my parents would take us camping in Minnesota and that continued when we moved to California. As I became an adult, I started doing a lot more hiking, which turned into peak-bagging. From there, I became interested in backpacking and that progressed to snow camping.
