My Days Are Numbered

Dream - October 22, 2025

I had a dream this morning that I was somewhere outside. I was looking up a hillside, there were two people near the top with Storm on a leash. And one of them said to me, "We had to take Shadow away from you because we know your time was short but we have brought Storm for you to give you comfort." Then, they unleashed her. As Storm was running down the hillside towards me, she was so happy and excited to see me!!... I was very sad and started crying uncontrollably because I never had the chance to say goodbye but there was this great divide between Storm and I, this black valley between us; and the blackness, this impenetrable darkness, encased me and then I woke up.

moved from MN to San Diego, CA - January 01, 1986
